I was born on September 29, 1995 in Lucknow, India. Nobody asked me whether I was OK with it, which I think was quite rude, but I made my peace with Arti and Sushil Madhuk.
We spent a few months in Lucknow, where my grandparents were marshalled into the act of baby raising, but packed up our bags and moved to Ahmedabad soon thereafter.
Parochial peacefulness would best describe Ahmedabad, but in spite of that, my parents found a group of hell-raisers to eat mutton and drink whiskey with. They were to become the lifelong buddies of my parents, essentially second sets of parents to me.
